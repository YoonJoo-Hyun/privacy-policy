02

Information We Collect

We do not collect information that directly identifies you (such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or photos).

Our apps may collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Device Information

  • Device model, operating system version, language and region
  • App version, performance and crash logs

2.2 Identifiers for Advertising & Analytics

  • Google Advertising ID (ADID) or similar identifiers
  • Used to show ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud

2.3 In-App Usage Data

  • Gameplay progress (levels, in-game currency, achievements)
  • Buttons or screens you interact with inside the app
  • Basic session information (play time, frequency of use)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive data such as political opinions, health information, or biometric identifiers.

03

How We Use the Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and operate our games and core features
  • Display ads, including personalized ads where permitted
  • Analyze usage to improve game balance, UX, and stability
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or abuse
04

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party SDKs and services, including but not limited to:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Unity Ads (advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics / Google Analytics for Firebase (analytics)
  • Google Play Services (basic platform functionality)

These third parties may collect and process data as independent controllers or processors in line with their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies for more details on how they handle data.

05

Data Sharing & Transfers

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information only in the following situations:

  • With third-party service providers (ads, analytics, crash reporting) solely to operate and improve our Services
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of PlayDay, our users, or others

Some of these providers may process data on servers located outside your country. In such cases, we rely on appropriate safeguards permitted by applicable laws.

06

Data Retention

We do not keep data on our own servers for longer than necessary. Most data is stored and retained by third-party providers such as Google and Unity under their own retention policies.

07

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the minimum age defined in the Google Play Store listing or under local law in your country.

If you believe that a child has provided data through our apps, please contact us. We will take reasonable steps to delete such information where required by law.

  • You can reset or limit the advertising ID in your device settings.
  • You may disable interest-based or personalized ads where the platform provides such options.
  • You can uninstall our apps at any time to stop all data collection by our apps.
